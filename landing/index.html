Privacy

Videos, thumbnails, transcripts, subtitles, reports, and stats are stored in the folder you choose during onboarding.

Local transcription

Whisper runs on your machine. The app can pre-seed model files for release builds.

You choose OpenRouter, OpenCode Go, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, or a LiteLLM proxy for report generation.
