Workflow

Record

Capture webcam and microphone sessions from the desktop app.

02

Transcribe

03

Analyze

Send the transcript to your selected model for structured critique.

04

Track

System

A private archive for deliberate practice.

01

Video journal

Record, review, save, and browse sessions without leaving the desktop app.

02

Whisper transcript

Timestamped segments become transcripts, subtitle files, and clickable review anchors.

03

AI critique

Grammar, fluency, clarity, reasoning, factual issues, and actionable improvements.

04

Progress memory

Recurring patterns, streaks, trend lines, and weekly rollups make progress visible.

05

Local-first archive

Sessions live in your journal folder as video, transcript, analysis, subtitles, thumbnails, and metadata.

06

Export tools

Generate subtitle tracks and export burned-in subtitled videos for later review.
